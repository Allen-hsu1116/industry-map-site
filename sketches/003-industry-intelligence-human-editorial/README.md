## Variant: Industry Intelligence Human Editorial IA

### Design stance
Use the selected Human Editorial direction as a whole-product information architecture: Daily Focus → Topic/Industry Chain Map → Company Detail.

### Key choices
- Daily Analysis is treated as an entry point, not the whole product.
- Industry map shows value-chain stages and company roles.
- Company detail separates fundamentals, technicals, chip/ownership, news/events, products, topic roles, and SWOT.
- Evidence/source metadata is present but secondary.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: product coherence and aistockmap-like navigation semantics.
- Weak at: still a static sketch; exact data paths/routes need implementation inspection.
